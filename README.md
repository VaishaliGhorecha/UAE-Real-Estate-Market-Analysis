📊 UAE Real Estate Market Analysis

🏙️ Project Overview

This project analyzes the UAE Real Estate Market using data analytics and interactive dashboards.

The objective is to help stakeholders:

✔ Understand market performance
✔ Identify high-value investment areas
✔ Analyze property demand and occupancy trends
✔ Evaluate property price & size distribution across cities

The project uses data visualization and business insights to support data-driven investment decisions.

🧰 Tools & Technologies
📊 Microsoft Power BI – Data Visualization
📑 Microsoft Excel – Data Cleaning
📈 Data Analysis
📉 Business Intelligence

🎯 Business Objectives

The goal of this analysis is to answer key real estate questions:

• Which UAE cities generate the highest property value
• Which locations provide strong investment opportunities
• What is the occupancy vs vacancy trend
• Which property types are most in demand
• How property prices vary across cities

🔍 Key Insights

📌 Dubai dominates the UAE real estate market with the highest property value and investment activity.

📌 Villas contribute the largest share of property value, indicating strong demand for larger residential properties.

📌 The market experienced major growth in 2024, showing increased investor confidence.

📌 Premium areas such as Dubai Marina, Al Furjan, Al Kifaf, and Dubai Creek Harbour have high property activity.

📌 Ajman and Al Ain have lower average property prices, making them attractive for affordable investments.

💡 Recommendations

✔ Focus investments in Dubai and Abu Dhabi due to strong demand and property value.

✔ Improve pricing strategies to increase occupancy rates.

✔ Explore affordable investment opportunities in Ajman and Al Ain.

✔ Monitor off-plan properties and upcoming developments.

✔ Prioritize high-demand locations such as Dubai Marina and Dubai Creek Harbour.
